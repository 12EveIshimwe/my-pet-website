How to identify cats

Learn about pedigreed cats:Pedigreed cats are bred by cat breeders and the cats have to meet certain breed standards according to the cat association with which they are registered.

Learn about random bred cats:Random bred cats are usually of unknown parentage and vary a great deal in size, body style, head type, ear and eye variation, tail length and type, coat length and density, colors and patterns, and personality.

Look at the cat’s face and eyes:Some breed types have very specific facial identifiers that may help you to identify a cat’s breed.
